Care Instructions

Webpal is a web-based personality.

By visiting and interacting with your Webpal, you are able to positively (or negatively) affect how it feels.

This shy (yet needy) friend likes to hide, but will show you how it feels in other ways.

It loves to see human faces and listening to you, but is sensitive to its surroundings so be wary.

You can keep up to date with its thoughts through the console feature in your web browser.
